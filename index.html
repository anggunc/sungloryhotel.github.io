Our Romantic Hotel's

A wonderfully secluded sanctuary beside Ubud's Ayung River, Mandapa, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve introduces hotel guests to another side of Bali. At this 5-star resort framed by verdant rice paddies and rolling hills, nature and culture infuse each experience.

Whether being led through an ancient healing session at the hotel's luxury spa, enjoying a cocktail crafted from Indonesian ingredients or dining on Balinese barbecue overlooking the rice fields of Ubud - locally inspired experiences abound.
